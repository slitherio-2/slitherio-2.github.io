How to play:

"Zombs Royale.io" is a fast-paced multiplayer battle royale game where you compete against other players to be the last one standing. Here's how to play: Enter the Game: Visit the game website and enter a username to start playing. You'll be matched with other players in a battle royale arena. Parachute In: You start the game by parachuting onto the map from a plane. Choose a landing spot and try to land near buildings to find weapons and loot. Collect Weapons and Supplies: Explore the map to find weapons, ammo, health packs, and other supplies. Pick up as much loot as you can to increase your chances of survival. Battle Other Players: Engage in combat with other players using your weapons. Stay inside the safe zone, as a deadly gas surrounds the map, shrinking the play area over time. Stay Inside the Safe Zone: The safe zone is marked on the map as a shrinking circle. Stay inside this circle to avoid taking damage from the gas. Survive and Eliminate Others: The goal is to be the last player or team standing. Eliminate other players while staying alive to win the game. Use Vehicles: You can find vehicles scattered around the map. Use them to move quickly and evade enemy fire. Watch Out for Airdrops: Occasionally, airdrops containing powerful weapons and supplies will appear on the map. Be cautious when going for them, as they attract other players. "Zombs Royale.io" offers intense battle royale action with a variety of weapons, items, and vehicles to use. Enjoy the thrill of the last-player-standing gameplay and strive to be the ultimate survivor!
