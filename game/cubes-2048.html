How to play:

Cubes 2048.io is an addictive and unique puzzle game that seamlessly combines the classic Snake and 2048 games! Are you ready to grow your snake as much as possible as you collect cubes and become the biggest and most spread snake on the screen? Move around the stage full of enemies while avoiding the big ones and eating the small ones. Combine similar figures to increase the size and number of your cubes and reach the highest score to achieve the final victory. Good luck! Who created Cubes 2048.io?
